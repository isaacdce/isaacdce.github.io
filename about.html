MEET THE INSTRUCTOR

Mr. Alvidrez: Has over 20 years in martial arts experience and has trained in multiple styles. Mr. Alvidrez studied the Jhoon Rhee system of Tae Kwon Do under Grandmaster Jose Santamaria. He continues his training with longtime friend and fellow martial artist, Master Gary Pruett of Pinnacle Martial Arts in Round Rock, TX.

Gain strength, courage, and confidence here at Alvidrez Martial Arts. Feel great about yourself while you achieve your fitness goals and master your mind, body, and soul. Anything can be accomplished with hard work and dedication and it is never too late to get in the best shape of your life. We strive to provide the mental strength you need to believe in yourself and in your abilities. Discipline and self control are very important qualities to instill in today's youth and adults. We offer classes for men, women, and children ages 6 and up. Parents, if you are looking for a way to develop self-esteem, discipline, and leadership for your children...this is the way.
